About Yvette

I am a Front-End Developer located in the greater Los Angeles area of Southern California.

I’ve always had a passion for web development. Since I had access to my first computer, a Windows 95, I’d be glued to the screen. I made my first website in 6th grade, around the same time that I taught myself basic HTML and CSS. In high school, I continued to study web and graphic design, gaining experience with Adobe Dreamweaver, Photoshop, and Illustrator.

Now, my main focus is in web design and front-end web development where I utilize my skills in CSS, HTML, Javascript, jQuery, to develop simple and modern responsive designs for apps and websites.
